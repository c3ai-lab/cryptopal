# User tutorial

## Register

<p>
    To register a new account click the sign up button on the landing page or just visit <a href="https://bluecryptopal.com/register" target="_blank">bluecryptopal.com/register</a>.
    Fill out the forms with your personal infomation. For merchants it is recommended to fill the optional company field for
    a better user experience while checkout.
    After hitting the register button you will receive a confirmation email to your provided email address. You have to
    confirm your email by clicking on the link in the confirmation mail. You are not able to use the service without confirming
    your email address.
</p>

## Dashboard

<p>
The main user page is the dashboard. It shows on the top left your last transaction, on default there should not be on listed.
If you have send or received your first transaction, it will occure here with the receiver/sender name, description, date and
value of the transaction. By clicking on it you can get more details.

Below it is your current balance displayed with a button to get some more balance. On the top right is a panel to send a
payment manually with additional shortcuts for the last up to three receiving contacts. By default there should not be anyone listed.

Below it is a list of the last three transactions referring to this account. It displays in and outgoing transactions in different
colors with the receiver/sender name, a description, the transaction date and the value. By clicking on a transaction you will get
more details of that specific transaction.
</p>

## Getting Liquidity

<p>
To get some balance to work with you have to click on the <i>Get Balance</i> button on the users dashboard. A modal will be opened
where you can enter an amount you would like to have. It has to be a maximum of \$1000.00 at a time. Now you can hit the button to
request the balance. You see a loding spinner for a short while. The blockchain transaction takes some time. If it is finished, you
will see the transaction hash and can close the window. Your balance on the dashboard should have been increased.
</p>

## Sending Payment

<p>
To send a payment you can click <i>Send Payment</i> in the navigation bar or click on the send button on the top right of the
dashboard. You can also use the shortcuts there, if you already have send some transactions. This will bring you to the send payment
page. Here you can enter an email address or a wallet address, a value you wan to send and a optional description.
If you hit the <i>Check Payment</i> button, the receiving address will be checked. If it is an CryptoPal user, you will see its name
and address. If its not, you will see a warning about an external transaction, which is not revertable. Confirm the payment by
clicking <i>Send Payment</i> button. You will see a modal with a loading spinner as long as your transaction is pending. After a short
amount of time you should see an success message.
</p>

## Reviewing Transactions
<p>
To get a receipt about your transactions you can click on a transaction on the dashboard or go to <i>Transactions</i> in the navigation
bar. There you see a list of all transactions belonging to your account. By clicking on one, you will get all details about this
transaction. If it is for an order, you will see a total order review here.

## External Checkouts

<p>
If you have the opportunity to pay with CryptoPal on an online shop, you select it as the payment method. When you click the buy button
you will be redirected to CryptoPal automatically. First you have to log in to your account by entering your CryptoPal credentials.
Then you see the order details, for which you have to pay. Click the Authorize Payment button to authorize a payment for that order.
Afterwards you have to confirm the final capture of the payment by clicking <i>Send Payment</i> button. After a short time you will
get feedback about the payment status. You can now redirect back to the shop.
</p>
