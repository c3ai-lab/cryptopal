# Merchant tutorial

In this section we describe all the additional functionality of a merchant. We describe how you can get the merchant status and
how to integrate CryptoPal to your shop and make it work. This is an additional section to the users how to where we describe
also things like register and basic functions of the CryptoPal platform.

## Change role

<p>
  If you want to be a merchant and use CryptoPal as your payment provider you
  have to change your role to merchant. You can easily do this by going to{" "}
  <i>Account Settings</i>, selecting <i>Change Role</i> and click on{" "}
  <i>Upgrade to Merchant</i> button. Now you are able to use CryptoPal as your
  payment provider on your shop by calling the api routes or using the
  WooCommerce plugin for your shop.
</p>

## Setup Woocommerce

<p>
  To set up CryptoPal as a payment provider for your woocommerce shop just
  download the <i>CryptoPal Payment Gateway for Woocommerce</i>
  plugin and have the merchant role on CryptoPal. Enable it and go to the settings.
  Here you just have to enter your email and password which you are using for logging
  into CryptoPal. Now the payment gateway should be working and you can receive payments
  via CryptoPal.
</p>

## Manage Products

<p>
  As a merchant you can select the <i>Products</i> section in the navigation
  bar. Here you can create, update or remove products form CryptoPal. You do not
  need to have products listed to sell them on your shop. Its an additional
  feature to link products directly.
</p>
